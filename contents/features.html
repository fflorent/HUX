HUX Modules

HUX is sort of a customable pack. Indeed, it is composed of :

  • A core, that each module will use as a framework
  • Modules, that you can add or remove, depending on your needs

If you want the whole pack of HUX, you can just download it from github.

Otherwise, you can just download HUX, and use the makefile (on *NIX machines only, sorry)
