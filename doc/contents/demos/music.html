JukeBox

This demo lets you play music titles. Click on pause at any moment :

  • The URL is updated, so the current time of the music is stored in it
  • if you refresh the page, you retrieve the music at the time you paused it
  • and obviously, you can share the URL with other people to let them listen a special part of a track ;)
